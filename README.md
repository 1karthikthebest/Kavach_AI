🛡️ KAVACH AI

AI-Powered Message Understanding & Decision Support

KAVACH AI helps users understand suspicious, confusing, or important messages and turn them into clear actions.

Built for social impact and responsible AI.

---

🚀 Problem

People receive hundreds of messages every day.

Many are:

- Confusing
- Misleading
- Stressful
- Difficult to act on

Users often do not know:

- What matters
- Whether action is required
- What to do next

KAVACH AI aims to make support easier to understand.

---

💡 Solution

KAVACH AI is an AI-powered assistant that analyzes text and transforms it into:

✅ Simple explanation
✅ Risk understanding
✅ Action suggestions
✅ Human-readable guidance

Instead of giving decisions, KAVACH AI helps users make informed decisions.

---

✨ Features

- 🔍 Analyze suspicious or unclear messages
- 🧠 AI-powered text understanding
- 📋 Step-by-step recommendations
- ⚠ Explainable reasoning
- 👤 Human-in-the-loop approach
- 🌐 Browser-based interface

---

🏗 Architecture

User Input
↓
Frontend (HTML + CSS + JavaScript)
↓
Gemini API Processing
↓
Analysis Layer
↓
Output & Recommendation

---

🛡 Responsible AI

KAVACH AI does not make final decisions.

Design safeguards:

- Human confirmation remains required
- AI outputs may be incorrect
- Recommendations include explanations
- Users stay in control
